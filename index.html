About Me

Axel Valero is a live sound engineer based in Mexico City, with over a decade of experience mixing concerts around the world. Specialized in FOH, Axel has toured extensively across the United States, Latin America, and Europe with artists such as Cuco, Caloncho, Belanova, León Larregui, and Fobia. He has delivered sound for major festivals and venues including Coachella, Primavera Sound, Vive Latino, Palnorte, Auditorio Nacional, and Palacio de los Deportes. With a musician’s ear and technical precision, Axel blends musicality and engineering to create unforgettable live experiences.
