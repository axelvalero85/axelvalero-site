About Me

Axel Valero is a live sound engineer based in Mexico City, with over a decade of experience mixing concerts around the world. Specialized in FOH, Axel has toured extensively across the United States, Latin America, and Europe with artists such as Cuco, Caloncho, Belanova, León Larregui, and Fobia.
